## Notes on sending cadence

Tie sends to the publish schedule: one email per new episode, day-of or next morning. Don't send more than that unless it's the launch issue or a genuine deep-dive — the brand promise is "only the truth," not "only your inbox." Three sends a month from a weekly upload schedule is about right (skip the announcement email when an episode is a smaller/secondary upload, and save the inbox space for the flagship weekly one).

**Platform note:** any of Mailchimp, Substack, or beehiiv can run these as-is. Substack/beehiiv are the lower-friction pick if you want subscribers to also browse past issues as a public archive; Mailchimp is better if you want tighter integration with paid placements down the line. Tell me which one you land on and I'll tailor merge-tag syntax (e.g. `*|FNAME|*` for Mailchimp vs `{{first_name}}` for beehiiv) into these templates.
