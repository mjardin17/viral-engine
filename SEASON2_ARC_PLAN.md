## LITTLE OLYMPUS — SEASON 2 (20 episodes)

### Season 2 Theme: "What Lives Below Comes Up"
Season 1 ended with the Titan settling in the Olympus Sea — watching the sky, not threatening, but present. Season 2 explores what it means to share your world with something ancient. New characters arrive. Relationships deepen. Zeus's father comes. And the next thing — bigger than the Titan — starts to stir at the edge of Season 2's finale.

### New Characters
- **Artemis** — Goddess of the hunt, Apollo's twin. Practical, competitive, bonds immediately with Achilles over precision. Introduced EP002.
- **Apollo** — God of the sun, music, poetry. Warm, theatrical, slightly too much. Introduced EP005.
- **Zeus's Father (Kronos)** — Not villainous — complicated. Has been imprisoned. His message "it's not time yet" was about his own situation. FINALE reveal.
- **The Titan (Oceanus)** — Not evil, just ancient. Starts communicating through the dolphins in EP006. Very slow. Very old. Wants to understand what the world became.

### Running Jokes (Season 2)
- **Artemis hits everything she aims at. Always. On the first shot. She does not think this is impressive.** (Plant EP002, recurring)
- **Apollo sings about everything. His songs are accurate but take three times as long as just saying the thing.** (Plant EP005, recurring)
- **The Titan (Oceanus) communicates through dolphins. The dolphins are trying their best. The translation is imperfect.** (Plant EP006, recurring)
- Existing jokes continue: Achilles heel arc evolves, Archie camera looks, Zeus crown, Medusa arc deepens

### Achilles Heel Arc (Season 2)
He told Zeus in EP019. The position was adjusted. In S2:
- EP003: First real training session where he trains differently because of the heel
- EP008: Athena notices a new pattern in the race data — the heel is getting worse, not better
- EP014: He asks Artemis about archery — something he can do that doesn't require running
- EP019: He learns to coach without demonstrating — teaches by watching, not by running
- EP020: Finale moment: his coordination role matters more than his speed. He's more useful than he's ever been.

### Medusa Arc (Season 2)
- EP004: Perseus visits the west garden. She shows him a sculpture she made of the eastern valley from his description.
- EP010: She sends a message asking if she can come to Olympus — just once, just at night, with mirrors. Everyone needs to decide.
- EP016: She visits. One night. It's complicated and beautiful.
- EP020: She fights alongside them in the finale. Her choice. Not asked.

## MECH LEGENDS — SEASON 2 (12 episodes)

### Season 2 Theme: "What You Don't Know You Can Do"
RUMBLE spent all of Season 1 learning what the team CAN do. His Season 2 question: "What can they do that they don't know they can do yet?" He's not trying to destroy them. He's trying to find the undiscovered ceiling — because whatever is coming in Season 3 will require it.

### Season 2 New Elements
- **RUMBLE's new proxy**: ECHO — a learning proxy that adapts mid-engagement, not scripted
- **NOVA's ancient contact**: SERA-1, a 3,000-year-old AI left by the civilization that originally built the Titan Array. She's not on anyone's side.
- **GRANITE's rebuilding**: His cryo-sleeve at zero. The rebuild takes all season. What he becomes is different from what he was.
- **ELDER-7's revelation**: He has memories the team needs — but they're locked behind trauma from the original sealing failure.

### RUMBLE Arc (Season 2)
He is now running a different kind of operation. He's testing, not attacking. Each episode: he engineers a scenario that forces one team member to discover an unknown capability. He logs it. He's building something — not a weapon. A profile. When the real Season 3 threat arrives, RUMBLE might not be the enemy.
