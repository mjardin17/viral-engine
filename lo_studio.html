🏛️ World Bible 5 Locations
The world of Little Olympus. Each episode should feel grounded in these locations and rules.
📜 World Rules
Age: All gods appear as children (ages 5-10). Zeus is 7, Hercules is 5, Athena is 8, Perseus is 7, Achilles is 7.
Powers: Everyone has their powers but they're unpredictable, not always on command, and sized down to kid-level consequences.
Adults: Only Uncle Hades and Poseidon appear as adults. They're bumbling, well-meaning, and can't quite handle children.
Tone: Warm, bright, funny, educational. No real danger. Stakes are real to the characters but always resolved safely.
Lesson rule: The lesson must emerge naturally from the story. No one lectures. Kids figure it out themselves.
Mini Medusa rule: She's dramatic, not scary. Her stone power is played for comedy. She almost always cancels her own threats.
No scene reuse: Every scene must be visually distinct. Check before generating.

🤝 Character Relationships
Zeus ↔ Hercules: Best friends. Zeus is louder, Hercules is stronger. They try not to compete but they do.
Zeus ↔ Athena: She's smarter. He knows it. He ignores her advice then regrets it. She says "I told you so" with a sigh.
Zeus ↔ Perseus: Zeus treats him like a sidekick. Perseus is actually the most thoughtful of the group.
Athena ↔ Medusa: Medusa respects Athena but won't admit it. Athena is the only one who speaks to Medusa like a person.
Achilles ↔ Hercules: Protective friendship. Achilles won't let anyone tease Hercules. Hercules thinks Achilles smells like lightning.
All ↔ Hades: He's the accidental babysitter. He signed up for none of this. He makes excellent tea. He loves them all.
Perseus ↔ Hermes: Hermes lent Perseus the winged sandals. He's been trying to get them back all season. Perseus "forgot" he has them.
Medusa's Snakes: Hissy (bossy), Slick (vain), Noodle (anxious), Chomp (big threats, no follow-through), Dreamy (asleep), Zigzag (restless), Tiny (wisest).
