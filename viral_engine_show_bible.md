## CHANNEL 1: LITTLE OLYMPUS

**Format:** Kids animated cartoon  
**Style:** Warm, colorful, funny, emotionally real  
**Episode length:** 63 seconds (7 scenes × 9s)  
**Tone:** Wisdom without being preachy. Comedy that earns its feelings.  
**Audience:** Kids 6–12, parents who watch with them

### The Cast
- **ZEUS** — King of Olympus, crown lists left when he has feelings (straight = significant, new record each time around Titan messages)
- **ATHENA** — Goddess of wisdom, keeps OBSERVATIONS (NOT FILED) notebook, Archie on her shoulder
- **ACHILLES** — Competitive, precise, measures things, writes in margins
- **ARTEMIS** — Had her own notebook since week 2, estimated Poseidon's fountain detour before the drill
- **POSEIDON** — Every note includes a sardine (habitual AND intentional), scenic route past the fountain
- **PERSEUS** — Carries things carefully (level), walks east, writes letters
- **HERCULES** — First to say yes, three careful sentences, lion skin hood
- **ARCHIE** — The owl. Slow blink. Notices everything first. The thing that makes everything possible.

### Secondary Characters (introduced S2)
- **MEDUSA** — Stone-gray skin, independent snake expressions (left=curious, right=skeptical), sculptor
- **HADES** — Three glasses system (dark=formal/departure, clear=administrative, purple=moved/annoyed), blue flame hair tells
- **CERBERUS** — Left head = THE CURIOUS ONE, one shared tail, tail wag affects all three heads
- **OCEANUS** — Older than the Titan, has been listening to every dolphin message since before the sea had a name
- **THE TITAN (C-0 equivalent)** — Underground, messages in ALL CAPS, "I HAVE TRIED NOT TO BE TOO HEAVY"
- **THE SNAKES** — SEVEN, THREE, FOUR, FIVE, TWO, ONE, RIVER (named after the underground river, "I decided. This took me eleven tries.")

### Season Structure
- **S1 (EP001–EP020):** Team forms, first correspondence with the Titan begins
- **S2 (EP021–EP040):** Titan arc — underground river discovered, correspondence deepens, Hades paperwork, RIVER gets her name, Titan sees Olympus at sunset, Achilles names his S3 arc
- **S3 (EP041+):** "Not the heel. The work." — Achilles's arc. Athena opens OBSERVATIONS — SEASON THREE notebook.

### Running Jokes (seed early, pay off hard)
- Zeus's crown lists left when he has feelings
- Crown goes straight = significant (new record for seconds straight around Titan messages)
- Poseidon's sardine in every note
- Archie's slow blink
- Dolphin translation is imperfect
- Athena's OBSERVATIONS (NOT FILED) notebook vs. official scrolls

### Viral Hook Formula
- Hook line establishes the unexpected premise
- Running joke appears in scene 2
- Emotional turn in scene 5
- Payoff in scene 7 that recontextualizes scene 1

## CHANNEL 3: MECH LEGENDS

**Format:** Action sci-fi, transforming mechs  
**Style:** Cinematic, dark, spectacular  
**Episode length:** 90 seconds (10 scenes × 9s)  
**Tone:** Against-impossible-odds. Team over individual. History as the enemy.  
**Audience:** 10+, action fans, Transformers-adjacent audience

### THE HERO TEAM

| Mech | Role | Color | Alt Mode | Character |
|------|------|-------|----------|-----------|
| BLAZE | Leader · Frontliner | Hot Orange | Tank | Bold and fearless. Charges first, inspires always. |
| GRANITE | Guardian · Defender | Slate Blue-Grey | Armored Truck | Unshakable and loyal. Protects the team and holds the line. |
| NOVA | Scout · Strategist | Cold Cyan Blue | Hover Bike | Fast and precise. Sees everything, plans every move. |
| STORM | Assassin · Infiltrator | Electric Purple | Stealth Jet | Silent and deadly. Strikes from the shadows. |
| ELDER-7 | Sentinel · Support | Ancient Bronze | Orbital Drone | Ancient and wise. Supports, scans and keeps the team alive. |
| RUMBLE | Bruiser · Demolitions | Crimson Red | War Hammer | Loud and relentless. Breaks through any obstacle. |

### Character Details
**BLAZE** (8m) — Smallest of the team physically but first in everything else. Amber energy core. Forward-lean posture. Readable expression. His "oh" moments are visible on his face. Jet transformation. First to move, last to stop.

**GRANITE** (12m) — Cryo-sleeve on left arm frosts when holding function activates. Cold exterior, warm amber core underneath. Geological strata armor. Immovable stance. Heavy armored vehicle. When GRANITE holds, the team holds.

**NOVA** (12m) — Holographic panels fold from forearms. Each channel = one panel. 17 panels deployed simultaneously = her true state. Satellite transformation. The 17-stream integrator. "The three-channel state feels like listening to one part of something. The full state feels like hearing what it's part of."

**STORM** (12m) — Electromagnetic arcs between joints. Motion blur capable. Stealth coating. Coiled spring mechanisms — always ready. Stealth jet transformation. 340% throughput increase when direction-signal function engages.

**ELDER-7** (40m) — Ancient. 2800+ years old. Deep red and black. Data pad always in hand. NEVER FULLY FACING FORWARD — always observing from an angle. Asymmetric (left side heavier). Orbital drone alt mode for surveillance. "Age is not weakness. It is accumulated war." Knows what every team before this one missed. Keeps the archive.

**RUMBLE** (28m) — Crimson red and black. UNKNOWN faction originally, now team member. Six interchangeable weapon loadouts. Asymmetric design (left heavier — shares this with ELDER-7, same lineage). Data pad. "Analyzing. Assessing. Preparing." Proved himself by doing what no one asked him to. Now he belongs.

### COLOSSUS
- 40m, Ancient Titan, UNKNOWN faction
- Weathered bronze and aged steel, amber glow in geological cracks
- Sealed for 3,047 years
- First expression: WONDER — not hostile, ancient and beyond measure
- "Wonder is the first expression. Age is its language."
- Role in S2: The signal from outside. RUMBLE is the answer. COLOSSUS was waiting to introduce them.
- S3: "Pending reply."

### THE VILLAIN ROSTER (GG Conquerors)
Each season of ML faces one historical conqueror from the GG villain row.  
The team must always look outmatched. Victory must feel against-impossible-odds.  
The key insight: every mech that faced these conquerors before faced them ALONE. That's why they all lost.

**S1: XERXES OF PERSIA** — The Immortal King  
2.5 million soldiers. The Persian Empire at full force. Thermopylae scale.  
Team of 6 vs. an empire. The hook writes itself.  
GG Historical S1 shows Xerxes's conquests before he appears in ML.

**S2: HANNIBAL BARCA** — The Carthaginian General  
Crossed the Alps with war elephants. Destroyed Roman armies at Cannae.  
Tactical genius who has already won before the battle starts.

**S3: CONSTANTINE XI** — The Last Emperor  
In this universe: the last defender who absorbed what destroyed him and returned as something else.  
Defender becomes conqueror.

**S4: GENGHIS KHAN** — The Great Khan  
Conquered more land than any human in history. Calm, absolutely certain.  
The composure of someone who has made decisions that ended civilizations.

**S5: SALADIN** — The Conqueror of Hearts  
Doesn't fight with force alone — fights with loyalty, faith, and the devotion of millions.  
The hardest to beat because you can't destroy what people believe in.

**S6: NAPOLEON BONAPARTE** — The Emperor  
Strategic genius. Reshaped the world. Lost at Waterloo — and came back.

### Season Arc Formula
- EP001: Villain activates. Team hears it. ELDER-7 identifies the threat. All six introduced.
- EP002–EP003: First contact. Team wins skirmish. Villain's scale becomes clear. They shouldn't celebrate.
- EP004–EP005: Each member tested individually. Their function matters. One nearly breaks.
- EP006–EP007: ELDER-7 opens the archive. This isn't the first time. Previous mechs all lost. Alone.
- EP008–EP009: Villain closes in. Team is outgunned. They hold because there's no choice.
- EP010: The insight. What every previous mech missed. This team has six.
- EP011: They try to act together perfectly. It's not enough. But it's closer.
- EP012: The answer. All six simultaneously. Against impossible odds, they hold. Villain retreats — not defeated. Will return. ELDER-7 updates the archive.

### Suppression Framework (S2 lore, still canon)
Undiscovered capabilities feel like system damage — self-suppressed.  
Unlocking requires stopping suppression, not activating something new.  
"What You Don't Know You Can Do."
