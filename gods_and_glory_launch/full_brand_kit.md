### Content strategy (TikTok ≠ YouTube — don't just repost full episodes)
TikTok needs short, punchy cuts, not 20-minute documentaries. For every full episode, carve out 3-5 short clips:
1. **The hook clip** (15-30 sec) — the cold open of the episode, ends on a cliffhanger, caption: "full battle on YouTube 🔗"
2. **The "wait, what?" fact clip** (20-40 sec) — one standalone surprising anecdote (e.g., "Hitler promoted this man specifically so he'd kill himself" — Stalingrad's Paulus story, or "This ship is still leaking oil 80 years later" — Arizona's Black Tears)
3. **The numbers clip** (15-20 sec) — fast-paced casualty/scale stats with bold on-screen text, high replay value
4. **The "history class never told you this" clip** — frame one anecdote as a myth-correction (these consistently outperform straight narration clips on TikTok)
